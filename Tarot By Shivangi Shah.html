Questions, answered

Before you book

What is a tarot reading?

Tarot is a divine tool used to gain insight into your past, present and future, or clarity about the people connected to you. With a few details from you, cards are drawn and deciphered through the reader's intuition so your questions can be answered.

What can I ask about?

Any and all questions about you can be answered through tarot — love, career, decisions, relationships, or simply what the cards want you to know. Within your booked time, questions are unlimited.

How accurate are the readings?

Honestly: no tarot reader can offer a 100% accuracy rate. Every reader has their own level of expertise and intuition, and accuracy varies accordingly. Readings are guidance — a light on the path, not a guarantee.

How are sessions carried out?

Regular and fast-track tarot readings happen via audio call or message on Instagram. Video consultations happen over Google Meet, Instagram video call or WhatsApp video call — whichever you prefer.

When are slots available?

Slots are taken every day from 11 am – 1:30 pm and 3 – 4 pm IST. DM on Instagram to know when the next slot is approximately available.

What if I miss my session?

Missing your session (including not being findable if you've changed your username) means the payment is forfeited. If Shivangi is late or misses the session by more than five minutes, you'll receive either a refund or a rescheduled slot as per availability.

What is the refund policy?

If you disagree with the booking conditions, you can apply for a refund within 12 hours of your confirmation message (1 hour for fast-track slots). Sigils, numerology charts and coaching are non-refundable once payment is made.

Can I record my session?

No — any communication recorded without explicit permission of @tarotbyshivangishah is a privacy violation and liable to legal action. Please be present, and take notes if you like.
